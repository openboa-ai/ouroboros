# Trading Modes

## Observer

- no real trading
- show hypothetical decisions, reasons, and state transitions

## Paper

- no real trading
- evaluate candidates against real market conditions
- keep paper candidates isolated from live execution

## Live

- explicit user enablement required
- full automatic trading allowed
- user can always intervene

## Control Surfaces

- global trading on or off
- symbol-level trading on or off
- force stop
- force close
- agent pause or stop
