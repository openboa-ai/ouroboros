# Lint Checklist

Use this checklist when health-checking a maintained wiki.

## Substance

- Is the wiki actually carrying knowledge, or only structure?
- Are there too many empty sections, directories, or templates relative to substance?
- Should some pages be merged rather than kept as thin fragments?

## Naming

- Is terminology consistent across maintained pages?
- Are old names still lingering after a rename?
- Do schema files and maintained pages use the same language?

## Content

- Are there duplicate pages covering the same concept?
- Are there contradictions between maintained pages?
- Are there claims with weak provenance?
- Are there useful answers still trapped in chat instead of filed back into the wiki?

## Connectivity

- Are there orphan pages with no inbound references?
- Does the index still reflect the main entry points?
- Are important pages missing from the index?

## Hygiene

- Is the log current?
- Are raw sources staying immutable?
- Is the schema helping the wiki, or overcomplicating it?
